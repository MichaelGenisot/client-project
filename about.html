Our Story

In 2022 we had a dream: create a recording studio that relieves all stress of studio recording, and allows for creative freedom. Our whole team of engineers have all been working on smaller projects at their home studios, but knew it was time for something bigger. So in 2023, the dream became a reality with a purchase of a studio space in New York. While only a few artists recorded in the first few months, word started to spread to New York musicians about world-class equipment, brilliant engineers, and a space that allowed for creative freedom. Since then we have continued to grow, and work with hundreds of artists.

Our Goal

Our goal is to have a studio that:

  1. Fosters an environment of creativity for all artists, allowing them to experiment and create a product they are satisfied with.
  2. Allows artists to determine how involved our team is, whether just with recording, producing, or having playing instruments.
  3. Provides a comfortable space for youth and young adults to use their gifts and record music in a way that is not stressful or intimidating.
  4. Benefits the community through events and giving back.

Our Studio

Our studio space was created with musical excellence and experimentation in mind. Our studio includes the latest equipment from Nord, Roland, DW, Fender, Gibson, Martin, Ibanez, Rode, and more. We have a wide range of musical instruments available in our studio as well, including acoustic guitars, solid-body and hollow-body electric guitars, bass guitars, keyboards and synthesizers, an upright and grand piano, and percussion instruments.
