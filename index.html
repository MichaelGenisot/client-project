Genesis Music Studio is a music recording studio designed to put the power of song writing and recording into the hands of the recording artists. The goal is to encourage young musicians to record music, and work with them to produce a product they are satisfied with. With top quality recording equipment and expert producers and engineers, Genesis Music Studio intends to make the recording and production process as comfortable as possible for recording artists, and being as involved or hands-off as the artists want. The Studio is located at 2112 52nd St. New York, NY.
